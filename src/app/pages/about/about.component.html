Ethiopia Federal Police Private Security Licensing

Licensing for Private Security Companies

To ensure public safety and maintain order, the Federal Police requires all private security companies to obtain a license. This rigorous process ensures that all companies adhere to the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct, safeguarding the well-being of individuals and communities. The licensing procedure involves several steps, starting with a comprehensive application process that delves into the company's background, experience, and qualifications.

Registration Process

The registration process for private security companies is designed to be straightforward and accessible, yet thorough. It involves submitting the required documentation, such as proof of company incorporation, insurance certificates, and other relevant credentials. This ensures that the Federal Police has a comprehensive understanding of the company's capabilities and its commitment to upholding security standards..
